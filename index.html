About Us

BRIC is recognized as a renowned multi-commodity Merchant Exporter in Bhubaneswar, ODISHA, India dealing in of all types of Goods from India & Abroad. Our Company registered with IEC Code from 2019, we are specializing in a variety of products and value added services. Bric has grown leaps and bounds and works with multi-nationals in India and across the world. We export various types of Goods with Large volume as a Merchant Exporter in India. The company has a full-fledged distribution network comprising from East, West, North and South of India and its logistics team coordinates accordingly to ensure timely supply of goods.
